ESTABLISHMENT

  • In 1991, a hostel for Orphans and Poor was established in Gopeng, Perak on 32 acres of land. The land and basic buildings are financed from the salary pool, service reward money and pension of the founder husband and wife, namely the late Cikgu Haji Din bin Kimi and Cikgu Hajjah Embun bte Muhammad Ali. The first session of student recruitment at this hostel began in 1992.

  • The experience of the two founders in the field of education opens up their desire to guide orphans and poor children based on monotheism to form and build people who are knowledgeable, have faith, have skills and have noble characters. This effort was also helped by their 11 children.

  • Until now, this complex is known as the Dahikmah Orphans and Poor Children's Welfare Organization which until now has 45 students, 3 childcare workers and 10 full-time staff.
