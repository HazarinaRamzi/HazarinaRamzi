OUR VISION

Make Dahikmah a hostel that cares and prioritizes the welfare and development of the potential of orphans and poor children so that they can succeed in this world and in the afterlife.


OUR MISION

  • Implement efficient, effective, transparent, and sincere management.

  • Generate the resources and facilities available to achieve success.

  • Creating opportunities and space for children to build their identity.

  • Empowering knowledge, charity and faith as a vehicle for children's success.

  • Foster family relationships and camaraderie among Dahikmah citizens.
