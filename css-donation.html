Construction of Dahikmah girls' dormitory

Since the establishment of Dahikmah, we have always been committed to helping orphans and the poor here by providing shelter and good education for the children. Until now, Dahikmah hostel only accommodates boys due to our limited facilities. However, God willing, with His permission, this year we intend to build another dormitory and provide the same facilities for girls. We believe there are still many less fortunate children out there who need help from all of us. Therefore, we are very pleased to invite the community together to help us in this struggle. For this construction, we need funds of 2.5 million.
